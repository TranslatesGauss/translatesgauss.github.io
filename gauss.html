The following list of Gauss' published works has been taken from the table of contents of his collected works. For each work, I intend to provide links to:
  1. the original work as it appears in the collected works,
  2. a LaTeX transcription of the original,
  3. any freely available English translations that I am able to find, and
  4. a lightly edited machine translation.
I do not speak Latin or German, so the machine translations should not be regarded as being definitive in any way. To report translation/transcription errors or suggest improvements, please send me an email. Of course, there are many works by Gauss that are not on this list because they are currently unpublished or were only published after his death (his "Nachlass"). In addition there is a vast amount of correspondence, some of which can be found in the collected works and some of which cannot.
